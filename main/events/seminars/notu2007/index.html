Nation of the Underground

July 23-27, 2007 - NOTU School of Heart and Soul at 7pm each night at Deliverance Bible Church - 310 W. Pipeline Rd. in Hurst TX.

Teachers include: Pastors Cleetus and Nichole Adrian from DBC Hurst and Pastor Skip Brooks from Rise Above Ministries in San Antonio, TX. The school will be mainly focused on subjects like deliverance and inner healing, and there will be personal ministry time each night.

The cost is $15 per person or $25 per married couples. And as with most NOTU events, senior pastors and their spouses are free. There will also be childcare provided each night for children 10 and under. There is no pre-registration for this event. It will be on a first come, first serve basis. Please pray about joining us for this amazing time with God.

More info at Nation of the Underground.
